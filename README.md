# Singapore EPSC Case Study Website

A beautifully designed, dark-themed interactive case study website for **"Designing Singapore's Employment Pass Services Centre"** — a service design case study centred on the Ministry of Manpower.

## 🇸🇬 About

This website presents the academic case study of Chu Wei Ming's project to design the EPSC (Employment Pass Services Centre) following the post-9/11 biometric registration mandate. It covers:

- The historical and policy context
- The contrasting demographics of WPSC vs EPSC
- The design approach and capacity planning
- Unique challenges faced by the project team
- The two pivotal Moments of Truth
- The management paradigm clash and Wei Ming's dilemma
- Key service design insights

## 🎨 Design

- **Theme**: Dark, premium, Singapore government-inspired
- **Palette**: Singapore red (`#EF3340`), deep black backgrounds
- **Typography**: Inter (body) + Playfair Display (headings)
- **Features**: Scroll reveal animations, counter animations, parallax glow, sticky nav
